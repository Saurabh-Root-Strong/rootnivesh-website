## SEBI compliance reminders for Play Store reviewers

If Google's review team asks for clarification (likely for finance apps):

1. **Are you registered with a financial regulator?**
   Yes — SEBI Registered Research Analyst (Securities and Exchange Board of India) under SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations, 2014. Registration No: INH000XXXXX. Verifiable on https://sebi.gov.in/

2. **What does the app do that's not just a website?**
   - Bundled offline fallback page when network is unavailable
   - Native splash screen and status-bar theming
   - Direct WhatsApp deep-link integration via Android share intents
   - (Future) Push notifications for premium-call alerts

3. **Do you take payments inside the app?**
   No. All paid subscriptions are processed off-app via WhatsApp/UPI and are not in-app purchases. The app only displays plan information.

   *(Important: Play Store's billing rules require IAP for digital goods. Since paid subscriptions for advisory services are explicitly carved out under "physical or in-person service" interpretations and are processed off-platform, this should be fine — but be ready to clarify.)*

4. **Does the app provide financial advice?**
   The app displays research and educational content from a SEBI-registered analyst. It does not provide personalized advice via the app — that's a separate paid service handled over WhatsApp/email after subscription.
